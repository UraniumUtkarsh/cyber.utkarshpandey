date: "March 2026"
description: "Security risks of using public WiFi networks"
---

Public WiFi networks are widely used in cafes, airports and hotels.

However, they can expose users to serious cybersecurity risks.

## Man-in-the-Middle Attacks

Attackers can intercept traffic on open networks.

They can steal:

- passwords  
- session cookies  
- banking data  

## Fake Access Points

Hackers create WiFi names like:

> Free Airport WiFi

Users connect unknowingly.

## How to Stay Safe

- Always use VPN  
- Avoid banking on public WiFi  
- Prefer HTTPS websites  
- Disable auto connect
